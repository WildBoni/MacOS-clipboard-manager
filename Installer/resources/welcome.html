ClipboardManager

A lightweight clipboard history manager for macOS. No dock icon, no menu bar clutter.

What gets installed

  • Binary → /usr/local/bin/ClipboardManager
  • LaunchAgent → ~/Library/LaunchAgents/

How to use it

  • Press ⌘ Shift V to open clipboard history
  • Navigate with ↑ ↓ or press 1–5 for quick pick
  • ↵ to copy the selected item  ·  ⎋ or click outside to close

History is stored in memory only — nothing is written to disk. Passwords from 1Password, Keychain, and other managers are never captured.
